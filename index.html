Token And Feedback Portal

How to Use This Portal?

  1. Scan the QR code using your phone camera or any QR scanner app.
  2. Once this page opens, enter your Child’s full name in the box.
  3. Select the Grade your child is studying in.
  4. Choose the Section your child belongs to.
  5. (Optional) — If you're visiting a subject-specific room like Science Lab, Computer Lab, or Library, you can select it from the dropdown.
  6. Click the “Generate Token” button.
  7. Wait a moment — you will receive a token number on the screen.
  8. Show this token number to the Teacher present at the venue.
  9. After the meet, click on the Feedback button below to submit your thoughts.

If you need help, please ask the nearest volunteer. We're here to assist you.
